What is LVOS

LVOS is the first densely annotated long-term video object segmentation dataset. LVOS aims to provide a benchmark for the development and assessing of long-term VOS models.

Long-term

Each sequence lasting 1.59 minutes on average.

High-quality and Dense Annotation

Pixel-wise annotations are available at 6 fps.

High Resolution

All videos are 720P .

Diverse Categories

27 categories with 7 unseen categories to simulate real application scenarios closely.

Lingual Labeling

Each sequence is provided with additional lingual label .

Large Scale

220 videos. 156,432 annotations.
